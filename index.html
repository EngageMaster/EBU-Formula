EBU. The Formula Of The Future - Storyboard

This presentation follows a non-linear path to showcase our journey and the EBU formula. Here's the rationale behind each step:

Slide 1: 2030 Vision

Why start here? To Hook & Inspire. Begin with the bold, aspirational end-state (full patient access) to immediately capture attention and frame the entire journey's purpose.

Slide 2: Market Context

Why this slide? To Contextualize. Ground the audience in the Oman CF market realities – size, budget, and critical patient journey gaps – setting the stage for the challenge.

Slide 3: 2022 Challenge

Why jump back? To highlight Urgency & Progress. Show the difficult starting point (copy treatments, lost tender) to emphasize the magnitude of our subsequent achievements.

Slide 4: 2023 Strategy

Why detail this? To Demonstrate Strategy ('E'). Explain our initial 'Efficiency' actions – segmentation and targeted early initiatives – showing a clear, data-driven plan.

Slide 5: 2024 RFQ

Why this milestone? To show Negotiation & Boldness ('B'). Highlight the first major breakthrough (full portfolio RFQ) and the bold high-level engagement required.

Slide 6: 2025 Win

Why celebrate? To showcase Success & Impact. Present the tangible results of our strategy – budget share, patient share growth, and patient numbers – proving the formula works.

Slide 7: EBU DNA

Why reveal now? To Cement the Formula. Explicitly define EBU = Efficiency • Boldness • You, connecting the journey's elements to our core operational DNA.

Slide 8: Call to Action

Why end this way? To Mobilize & Empower ('U'). Rally the team, acknowledge their pride, and issue a clear call to action, emphasizing their crucial role ('U') moving forward.

Section: “Slide Rationale”
  • Objective: Explain the narrative flow and purpose of each slide to stakeholders/managers.
  • Layout: Grid layout (2 or 3 columns) of rationale cards.
  • Card Style: Standard card with thicker left border matching the primary color theme of the slide it describes.
  • Content: Each card has Slide #/Title and a brief "Why this slide?" explanation focusing on the key objective (Hook, Contextualize, Urgency, Strategy, Boldness, Success, Formula, Mobilize).
  • Icons: Use relevant icons for each rationale point (e.g., chevrons-right for Vision, map for Context, alert-triangle for Challenge, etc.).
  • Animation: Simple fade-in for each card, staggered.
  • Focus: Justify the non-linear presentation structure and the strategic importance of each content block.
